About

Hi, I'm Thairlan, 26 UX Designer, passionate about software development, three hundred and fifty page books, fluffy cats, and coffee.

I'm a proud member of the LGBTQIA+ community, and founder of UX Rainbow, a web-based social inclusion initiative that aims to include people in the world of UX Design.

I fell in love with UX Design when I discovered that through empathy, communication, and adaptability I could contribute to designing more equitable solutions.

I am also self-taught, I measure no effort to research and go where the knowledge is, I love to listen and teach, from my point of view everyone has something to teach, and everyone has something to learn.

There is a quote by American entrepreneur Steve Jobs, that I take as a reminder, that guides me to my goals in life:

"Your time is limited, so don't waste it living someone else's life." - Steve Jobs.
